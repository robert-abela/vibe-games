# Bike Around

A fun, kid-friendly browser game where you ride a scooter to visit your grandmas, dodging cars and police along the way!

## Gameplay
- **Objective:** Safely reach your grandma's house (or home) by riding your scooter down a busy road, avoiding obstacles and taking the correct exit.
- **Controls:**
  - Use the **Left (⬅️)** and **Right (➡️)** arrow keys to change lanes.
  - Press **Space** or click **Start** to begin or restart the game.
  - You can also tap/click the game area to start or restart.
- **Obstacles:**
  - Cars and police cars appear in random lanes. Colliding with them ends the game.
  - Police cars trigger a siren sound and have a special crash animation (jail).
- **Levels:**
  - There are 3 levels, each with a different destination (Anne, Marianne, Home), increasing speed, and more obstacles.
  - Each level ends with a greeting scene at your destination.
- **Exits:**
  - As you approach your goal, an exit appears on the left or right. You must be in the correct lane to take the exit.
  - Missing the exit rewinds you slightly so you can try again.

## Features
- **Kid-friendly, top-down graphics** with bright colors and simple shapes.
- **Sound effects** for lane changes, crashes, sirens, and level completion.
- **Animated obstacles** and fun greeting scenes at each destination.
- **Progress indicators** for level, goal, and distance.
- **Responsive controls** for keyboard and pointer/touch.

## How to Play
1. Open `index.html` in your browser.
2. Click **Start** or press **Space** to begin.
3. Use ⬅️/➡️ to dodge cars and police.
4. Watch for the exit sign and move to the correct lane to reach your goal.
5. Complete all levels to win!

## File Structure
- `bike-around/index.html` — Main HTML file for the game.
- `bike-around/js/game.js` — Game logic and rendering (this file).
